The Grammar

verb + motion = action

Verbs

dDelete
cChange (delete + insert)
yYank (copy)
vVisual select
> / <Indent / Unindent
gu / gULowercase / Uppercase

Examples

d + w = delete word
c + i" = change inside quotes
y + $ = yank to end of line
> + 5j = indent 5 lines down

Tip: Double a verb to act on the line: dd yy cc

Motions

Horizontal

eEnd of word
0 / $Start / end of line
^First non-blank

Vertical

Combination Matrix

w iw $ i" i{ it 5j
d dwto next word diwword d$to EOL di"in quotes di{in braces ditin tag d5j5 lines
c cwto next word ciwword c$to EOL ci"in quotes ci{in braces citin tag c5j5 lines
y ywto next word yiwword y$to EOL yi"in quotes yi{in braces yitin tag y5j5 lines
v vwto next word viwword v$to EOL vi"in quotes vi{in braces vitin tag v5j5 lines

The math: 6 verbs × 10 motions = 60 commands. Add i/a variants = 120. Add counts = infinite.
